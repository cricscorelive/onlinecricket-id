From available marketing materials, both claim competitive odds. Users sometimes report Diamond as slightly more cricket-specialised.

Can I switch between platforms if one doesn’t satisfy?

Yes—you may create an ID on one platform and later get another on the other platform; however check deposit/withdrawal requirements.
